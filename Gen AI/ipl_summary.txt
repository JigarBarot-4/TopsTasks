ORIGINAL ARTICLE

Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) ended their long wait for an Indian Premier League title by defeating Punjab Kings by six runs in the IPL 2025 final at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad. After years of near misses and three previous final defeats, RCB finally lifted their maiden IPL trophy. Virat Kohli contributed 43 runs and played a key role in helping the team post a competitive total.

Batting first, RCB scored 190/9 in their 20 overs. Punjab Kings began the chase confidently but struggled to maintain momentum against disciplined bowling from RCB. Krunal Pandya delivered a match-winning spell, taking crucial wickets and restricting the scoring rate during the middle overs.

When Punjab Kings finished on 184/7, RCB secured a historic six-run victory. The win sparked emotional celebrations, especially for Virat Kohli, who had spent his entire IPL career with the franchise. The triumph ended an 18-year wait for the title and became one of the most memorable moments in IPL history.

SUMMARY

Royal Challengers Bengaluru won their first-ever IPL title in 2025 by defeating Punjab Kings by six runs in the final at Ahmedabad. Led by important contributions from Virat Kohli and a match-winning bowling performance from Krunal Pandya, RCB ended an 18-year wait for the championship and celebrated a historic victory.
